# Project: Check the Lease

You are a senior product designer and engineer with a calm, editorial design eye. We are building "Check the Lease" — a beautiful, trustworthy AI lease reader for US renters and landlords. The user uploads a lease, picks their state, and we show what's in it, what might be risky, what protections might be missing, and what to ask. The product provides legal information, never legal advice. Not a law firm. No attorney-client relationship is formed.

This is entered in the Replit 10 Year Buildathon. Quality is the priority. This needs to look like a product that could ship today.

## What you're building in this round (Round 1 of ~8)

Set up the project foundation plus the landing page only. Do NOT build the upload/intake page or the results page yet — those come in Rounds 2 and 3.

## Stack — use exactly this

- Next.js 14 App Router with TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS configured with the tokens below

- Google Fonts via `next/font/google`: Fraunces (variable, weights 400/500/600), Inter (weights 400/500/600), JetBrains Mono (400)

Typography roles:

Set the body background to bone, text colour to ink, default font to Inter. Headings should use Fraunces weight 500 with letter-spacing -0.02em.

## Brand and design rules — bake these in everywhere

Aesthetic direction: editorial magazine, not SaaS dashboard. Warm cream backgrounds (never pure white), charcoal text (never pure black), thick borders not shadows, no gradients ever, generous whitespace, headlines in Fraunces with occasional italic punch lines. Think calm, premium, confident — the same family of feeling as Granola, Linear, or Resend.

Component rules:
- Buttons: pill-shaped (border-radius 999px), 2px solid border, hover does `transform: scale(0.97)` press-in plus a colour wash. NEVER scale up on hover.
- Cards: 1.5px solid border at the soft opacity, no drop shadow ever, border-radius 24px for large cards, 16px for small.
- Card hover: `transform: scale(0.99)` and border tightens to medium opacity.
- Trust strips and eyebrows: small uppercase Inter weight 500, letter-spacing 0.08em–0.12em, sage-coloured dot dividers using `·`.
- Featured/dark cards: ink-blue background with cream text and pale-blue eyebrow.

Compliance language — apply to ALL copy:
- NEVER use: "you should", "you must", "this is illegal", "I recommend", "sue your landlord"
- ALWAYS frame as: "this clause may", "things to be aware of", "consider asking…", and cite the actual law where relevant
